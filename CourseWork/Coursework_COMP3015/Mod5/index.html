Cat Care Essentials

It's very important that you know the essential care things you'll need in order to take proper care of your new cat. Below is a list of things that you need to keep in mind when shopping for your new pet. Remember that this is only a suggestion of the bare essentials and you can always spoil them more!

  1. Set up a safe home area with no open windows or doors that lead outside
  2. Purchase healthy cat food and create a feeding schedule for your cat. Please do not free-feed your cat
  3. Prepare a water bowl with clean water
  4. Set up a litter box in an accessible area and fill it with cat litter
